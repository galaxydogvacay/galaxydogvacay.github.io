Day Care

During the day, your dog will get plenty of love and attention. They are allowed to roam freely in the house. We take them out for walks as often as they need them. For some dogs, it may be once every couple of hours. Other dogs prefer to stay indoor and that is fine with us too. Please bring food and treats for your dog. We also have a supply of dog treats if you allow us to serve your dog.

We pride ourselves in caring for your dog and will send you updates about your dog (with photos) during the day. Our dog daycare comes with a free doggy portrait session while your dog is with us.
